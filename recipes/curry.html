Curry

Description

Fragrant, slightly spicy Punjabi butter chicken is also known as murgh makhani. This authentic Indian curry consists of tender pieces of marinated char-grilled chicken in a silky smooth, creamy butter and tomato sauce. We've compiled this collection of restaurant-worthy recipes for you so you can recreate the amazing flavors of butter chicken at home!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Spices
  • Cream

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken
  2. Cook the chicken
  3. Cook the tomatoes and spices with the onion and garlic
  4. Add the chicken and cream
  5. Let simmer until finished
